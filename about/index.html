About OpenEPCIS

OpenEPCIS is open-source reference implementation developed and maintained by Benelog Gmbh which is based on EPCIS standard and offers various projects, tools and artifacts.

The GS1-developed EPCIS standard offers supply chain visibility for goods, logistical units, and other assets.
