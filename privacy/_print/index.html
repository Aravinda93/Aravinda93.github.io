What are your data protection rights?

Our Company would like to make sure You are fully aware of all of Your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to obtain copies of Your personal data from Our Company.

The right to rectification - Any information that you think is incorrect may be requested to be corrected by Our Company. Additionally, you have the right to ask Our Company to fill any information that you feel is lacking.

The right to erasure - Under certain circumstances, you have the right to ask Our Company to delete Your personal data.

The right to restrict processing - Under certain circumstances, you have the right to request that Our Company restrict the processing of Your personal data.

The right to object to processing - Under some circumstances, you have the right to object to how Our Company is using Your personal information.

The right to data portability - You have the right to ask Our Company to provide the information we have acquired directly to you or to another organization in certain circumstances.

In the event that you submit a request, we have one month to answer. To exercise any of these rights, please get in touch with us at privacy@openepcis.io.
