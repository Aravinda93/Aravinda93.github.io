Do we collect any data?

Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information are not collected by us, either directly or indirectly during the usage of the OpenEPCIS.io service.

You can contact us via email privacy@openepcis.io to find out more about Our services or offers. This is the only way we can get your information (email-id). Your email addresses and the other details you sent in the email are momentarily retained in for responding to your queries.

No data is gathered by OpenEPCIS.io, and none is used for analytics or performance improvement. Additionally, none of the data or information is sent to external entities.
 

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to obtain copies of your personal data from benelog GmbH & Co. KG.

The right to rectification - Any information that you think is incorrect may be requested to be corrected by our company. Additionally, you have the right to as to fill any information that you feel is lacking.

The right to erasure - Under certain circumstances, you have the right to ask for deletion of your personal data.

The right to restrict processing - Under certain circumstances, you have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.

The right to object to processing - Under some circumstances, you have the right to object to how we are using your personal information.

The right to data portability - You have the right to ask benelog GmbH & Co. KG to provide the information we have acquired directly to you or to another organization in certain circumstances.

In the event that you submit a request, we have one month to answer. To exercise any of these rights, please get in touch with us at privacy@openepcis.io.
